## Common Pitfalls & Solutions

### Pitfall 1: Quote Stuffing
**Problem**: Adding low-quality quotes to meet word count
**Solution**: Quality-first approach - reject sources that don't meet EQS â‰¥3

### Pitfall 2: Analytical Starvation
**Problem**: 20% quotes leaves insufficient space for analysis
**Solution**: Integrated writing - analysis and quotes in same sections

### Pitfall 3: Single Perspective
**Problem**: All quotes from similar viewpoints
**Solution**: Diversity audit - ensure representation of scholarly debates

### Pitfall 4: Decorative Quotes
**Problem**: Quotes included for volume, not substance
**Solution**: Purpose audit - every quote must materially advance argument
