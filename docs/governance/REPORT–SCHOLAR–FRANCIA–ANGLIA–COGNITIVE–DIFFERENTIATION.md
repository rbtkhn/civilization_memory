## I. Executive Summary

The two Scholar files encode **distinct civilizational epistemes**: Francia’s Scholar is an **accumulative learning ledger** (Phase I) that records beliefs and syntheses with proposed RLLs and explicit axioms; Anglia’s Scholar is a **locked read-only ledger** (no Phase designation) that records doctrines and a single heuristic—exit vs absorption. The differentiation is “cognitive-spiritual” in the sense that each file embodies a different **relation to authority, continuity, and failure**—Francia through declaration, rupture-as-renewal, and epistemic gaps (NCZ); Anglia through procedure-as-memory, defeat-without-collapse, and **structural opposition** (“Francia fails when forced to exit; Anglia fails when forced to absorb”).
