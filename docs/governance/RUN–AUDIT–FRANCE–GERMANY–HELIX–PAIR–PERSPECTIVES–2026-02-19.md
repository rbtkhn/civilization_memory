I. EXPECTED PERSPECTIVE FRAMEWORKS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

**Scholar-France (Francia):**
- Declarative legitimacy; principle → law → institution
- Universalism-first; rupture-as-renewal; centralization-as-coherence

- Encoding: administrative victory, restraint, stopping rules, limits of competence
