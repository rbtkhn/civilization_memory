## II. Transcript-Derived Marker Inventory

### A. Opening / Framing (Program Mode)

- **"Good day. Today is Tuesday 10th March 2026."** — Exact date/topic opener per profile ("Good day. Today is [date], and [topic]...").
- **"And before I proceed with this program, let me remind you again to tick the like button..."** — Channel housekeeping before substantive content.
- **"Well, over the last 24 hours, um, we have seen a whole spate of contradictory messages and signals... which cumulatively do seem to suggest growing doubts..."** — Topic then difficulty/careful framing.
- **"One needs to be extremely careful what one says about all of this. However, because one also gets messages which still seem to imply... a decision to seek further escalation."** — Scope/care; tensions preserved (off-ramp vs escalation).
- **"But anyway,..."** — Recurring transition (multiple instances).
- **"And in light of this,..."** — Listed transition phrase.

- **"as far as I'm aware"** / **"to my knowledge"**

- **"I would say"** (that in relation to this particular conversation...)

- **"I don't know whether or not it is true"** / **"It may be true"** / **"wouldn't surprise me if it is"**

### C. Precision / Scope / Deferral

- **"For the record,"** — (I don't believe him; I think that any operation to unblock the Straits of Hormuz... is so dangerous... that I don't personally think the United States is going to attempt it at all.)
- **"I discussed this whole topic at length yesterday. I'm not going to repeat what I said."**
- **"All I would say again is that..."**
- **"Just saying."** — Multiple instances (dismisses or limits claim without elaborating); e.g. after Ushakov/sincerity point, after Netanyahu calculation, after mediators not proposals, after Russians not guaranteeing outcome, after sanctions not lifted yet.
- **"I am not going to discuss the technology of this, which is again not my thing."**
- **"Now I am not going to explore in this program why..."** — Not exact but same function (limits scope).
- **"Just saying."** — Recurring deferral.
- **"we are not yeah there yet and perhaps we never will"** — Non-closure; scope limit on conclusion.

### F. Conclusion / Non-Closure

- **"we are not yeah there yet and perhaps we never will"**
- **"Well, a lot of very heavy analysis in this program this is where I'm going to finish today's program"**

### G. Interpretive "In Effect" / Reading of Signals

- **"What the government that publishes the fact is telling us is that the government that initiated the call came begging and that clearly is what happened in this call between Trump and Putin."**
- **"the word frank can often be taken to mean in Russian readouts... that the conversation was contentious, perhaps even heated at times."**

- **"In other words, Trump complained to Putin that Putin up to now... has not been willing to speak to him. That's my interpretation of these words. But isn't it the obvious one?"**

### H. Qualified Acknowledgment / Personal View vs Outcome

- **"It may be that things will indeed work out that way. I personally think that..."** — Profile pattern (personal view vs possible outcome).
- **"I don't know whether the Iranians would accept that. I don't know whether the supreme leader... would be prepared to agree... I suspect it would be a very tough sell."**
- **"Perhaps a sense of realism is growing."** — Hedged; then evidence (WSJ, Israel reports, president's comments).

### J. Absent (No Bleed)

- **No "How could anybody possibly believe...?"** or **"Who in the heck...?"** (Mearsheimer/Barnes).
- **No Barnes markers:** no constraint hierarchy, no sobriquets, no "One issue is... Then the second..."
