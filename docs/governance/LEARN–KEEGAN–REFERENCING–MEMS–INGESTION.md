INDIA: Keegan documented for methodological/contrast use in CIV–SCHOLAR–INDIA; not added to ARC–INDIA (RESEARCH–KEEGAN–ALEXANDER–INDIA–COMMAND–FRAMING).

**2. Recurring Keegan themes in MEMs**  
• "Civilised warfare" and institutional restraint; Clausewitz "little of oriental war"; limits of Clausewitzian rationality.  
• Mask of command: leadership as performance; Napoleon (magnetism) vs Wellington (competence); belief collapse when commander departs or fails.  
• Face of battle: soldier experience, confusion of close-quarters combat, defensive-infantry vs combined-arms.

**3. Scholar governance**  
• **Islam:** SYNTHESIS 0005 (five-pole pool: Gibbon, Lewis, Phillips, Kissinger, Keegan). RLL–ISLAM–0003: for war/command/battle MEMs, Keegan recommended for civilization-contrast; "repository corpus, not ARC–ISLAM."  
• **Persia:** Keegan in ARC–PERSIA; Scholar uses him for Carrhae (civilisation-contrast) and Darius III (belief collapse).  
• **India:** Keegan not in ARC–INDIA; RESEARCH doc limits use to command-as-performance framing for Alexander/India MEMs.
