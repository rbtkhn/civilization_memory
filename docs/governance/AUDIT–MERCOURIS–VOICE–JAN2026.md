## I. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The transcript **strongly validates** the existing Mercouris linguistic fingerprint. Constant epistemic hedging, reset pivots (“But anyway,” “Anyway,” “So anyway,” “Well, there it is”), reference to prior programs, temporal framing, double-negative parsing, historical analogy (Hapsburg/Serbia 1914), controlled affect breach (“makes my heart sink”), scope limitations (“I’m not going to explore”), and narrative geometry (frame → evidence → interpretation → qualification) all appear in natural, sustained use. **Gaps:** several recurring phrases and structural patterns in the transcript are not yet in the profile and should be added. **Over-specification:** a few profile markers (e.g. “Now about this…”, “So let’s put all that aside and look at…”, “The short answer is…”) do not appear in this transcript; they remain valid as optional markers from other appearances.

**Verdict:** Profile is **authentic and realistic** for this sample. Recommended refinements are additive (new markers and one structural pattern).
