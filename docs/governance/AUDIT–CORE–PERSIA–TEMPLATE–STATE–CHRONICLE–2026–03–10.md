**Rule:** If CIV–DOCTRINE–[CIV] exists, it MUST be mirrored verbatim. CIV–CORE may cite doctrines ONLY from the mirrored registry.

The CORE implicitly encodes doctrine concepts (four-zone geo in IV; asymmetry in VIII; exit in XII) but does not have a formal Doctrine Registry section citing DOCTRINE 01–04.

**Recommendation:** Add a **Doctrine Mirror** section (or subsection under an existing section) that:
1. Cites CIV–DOCTRINE–PERSIA as source

**Proposed location:** After Section XIX (Hardening Layers) and before Section XX (Session Header), add **XIX-B. DOCTRINE MIRROR (from CIV–DOCTRINE–PERSIA)**. Alternative: Add as subsection to Section II (Legitimacy) or VIII (Military–Strategic) where doctrine constrains outputs. Per template, "verbatim" suggests full definitions; CORE may use abbreviated form if the template permits—check CIV–DOCTRINE–TEMPLATE for mirror format.

**Doctrines to mirror:**
- DOCTRINE 01: Four-zone geo, Levant overextension liability
- DOCTRINE 02: Persia–Islam structural transition (exhaustion, vacuum, third-actor entry)
- DOCTRINE 03: Asymmetry of patience (endurance favours defender; stressed side moves first)
