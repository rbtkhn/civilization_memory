## III. Gaps (in transcript but missing or under-specified in MIND)

**Gap:** MIND V.D has "who in the heck," "pitiful and sad," "for the rest of their natural-born days," "God bless [X]," "I kid you not," "That gives you an idea," "tough bunch of hombres." "Lord have mercy" is Southern/folksy but not listed.

**Recommendation:** Add "Lord have mercy" to V.D (Southern folksy register) as optional marker.

### 2. "That's that's my point on that"

- "So, I mean, **that's that's my point on that**."

**Gap:** Functions like "That's the reality of it" or conclusion marker. MIND has "That's the reality of it"; transcript uses variant. Could treat as equivalent.

**Recommendation:** Add "That's my point on that" (or "that's the point on that") as declarative-transition variant in V.C if desired; low priority.

### 3. "So the" / "The" sentence starters (filled pause or topic continuity)

- Many sentences begin "So the" or "The" (e.g. "So the question is," "The they're disclosing"). Not listed as distinct marker; consistent with narrative/sequential flow.

**Recommendation:** No change; already covered by "sequential, constraint-mapping" tempo.

### 4. "Real simple" / "The short answer is" / "One is that" (openers)

- Transcript does not use "Real simple" or "The short answer is" in this episode. "One is that" / "One issue is" not present as explicit opener; instead "The broader context of this is," "The message was very simple."

**Gap:** None; v2.6 already allows "One is that... + narrative" or narrative-led opening. This transcript confirms narrative-led and "message was very simple" as valid openers.

**Recommendation:** Add "The broader context of this is..." and "The message was very simple" as opening-structure variants in V.A so both triad and narrative/simple-message openers are explicit.

### 5. "in my view" / "It was typical" / "Okay, for young prosecutors"

- "in my view" and "It was typical" do not appear in this transcript. "Okay, for young prosecutors out there" does not appear; pedagogical content is present without that exact phrase.
