I. PURPOSE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This document maps Churchill’s **Volume 3 (*The Age of Revolution*)** to
existing Anglia MEMs and proposes **verbatim quote integration** as
civilizational evidence (ARC-T-MODERN, ERC-SECONDARY). Vol. 1 (*The
Birth of Britain*) and Vol. 2 (*The New World*) are already integrated
in multiple MEMs; Vol. 3 covers roughly 1688–1815 (Marlborough,
Succession Wars, Utrecht, Seven Years’ War, American and French
Revolutions, Napoleon, Trafalgar, Waterloo) and is **not yet cited** in
the repository.
