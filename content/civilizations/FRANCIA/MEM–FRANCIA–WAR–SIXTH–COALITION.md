I. MEMORY PURPOSE & SCOPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This file preserves the War of the Sixth Coalition as **the decisive
systemic reversal** in which Francia’s military excellence can no longer
compensate for legitimacy loss, coalition convergence, and resource
exhaustion.

Defeat is no longer avoidable.
Collapse becomes structural.

Contradictions are preserved without synthesis.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The Russian campaign annihilates core capacity.

Conditions:

Fear dissolves.

The Sixth Coalition encodes **the failure of excellence without
legitimacy**.

Lessons:

• Capacity collapse accelerates legitimacy loss.

• Francia’s model fails when belief drains faster than force.

This is the terminal Napoleonic war.
