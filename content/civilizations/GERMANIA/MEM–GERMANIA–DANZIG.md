I. MEMORY PURPOSE & SCOPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This file preserves Danzig as **Germania’s most volatile corridor
symbol**: a city where legal ambiguity, national identity fracture,
and external guarantees combined to convert grievance into war trigger.

For Germania, Danzig is not a lost city.
It is **a denied continuity point**.

Contradictions are preserved without synthesis.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
II. HISTORICAL ROLE AS HANSEATIC NODE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Danzig emerges as a German commercial city.

Functions:
• Hanseatic League port
• Grain-export hub
• German-speaking mercantile elite
• Autonomous municipal tradition

Identity formation:
• Commercial
• Germanic
• Self-governing

The city’s legitimacy rests on trade, not sovereignty.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
III. POLISH–GERMAN CONTESTED SPAAD
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Over centuries, Danzig shifts between spheres.

Dynamics:
• Polish crown suzerainty with autonomy
• German cultural predominance
• Economic integration with hinterland
• Political ambiguity normalized

Danzig survives through **legal exceptionality**.

Ambiguity becomes habit.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IV. VERSAILLES SETTLEMENT & LEGAL FRACTURE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1919 transforms Danzig into a liability.

Settlement features:
• Free City under League of Nations
• Customs union with Poland
• Separation from Germany
• Corridor severing East Prussia

From a German perspective:
• National continuity is broken
• Self-determination denied
• Corridor imposed for strategic containment

Legal neutrality replaces organic integration.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
V. CORRIDOR PSYCHOLOGY & NATIONAL HUMILIATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Danzig becomes symbol, not asset.

Effects:
• Focus of nationalist grievance
• Evidence of Versailles injustice
• Corridor obsession in German politics
• Emotional condensation point

The city is small.
The symbolism is enormous.

Danzig converts geography into ideology.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VI. DIPLOMACY FAILURE & ESCALATION DYNAMICS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Attempts at negotiated revision fail.

Constraints:
• Polish security dependence
• Allied guarantee rigidity
• League of Nations impotence
• German impatience with legal delay

The Free City becomes:
• Ungovernable
• Over-symbolized
• Strategically brittle

Ambiguity no longer stabilizes.
It destabilizes.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VII. 1939 ABSORPTION & WAR IGNITION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
German annexation of Danzig coincides with invasion of Poland.

Meaning:
• Legal ambiguity resolved by force
• Corridor reclaimed militarily
• European war triggered

The city becomes the **spark**, not the cause.

Danzig demonstrates how unresolved legitimacy disputes ignite systems.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VIII. POST-1945 ERASURE & FINALITY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
After 1945:
• Danzig becomes Gdańsk
• German population expelled
• Memory severed
• Border rendered irreversible

Germania’s connection ends completely.

No ambiguity remains.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IX. CIVILIZATIONAL MEMORY FUNCTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Danzig encodes **the danger of frozen legal contradictions**.

Lessons:
• Corridors magnify grievance.
• Legal neutrality cannot substitute for legitimacy.
• Symbolic cities distort strategic judgment.
• Ambiguity stabilizes only when time pressure is low.
• Unresolved borders invite catastrophic resolution.

Danzig is Germania’s escalation caution.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
X. CONTINUITY INSIGHTS (RAW)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Small territories can carry system-breaking symbolism.
• Legal solutions fail when identity is excluded.
• Corridor logic accelerates conflict.
• Delay without resolution radicalizes claims.
• Danzig is Germania’s warning against frozen settlements.

Tension preserved without verdict.
