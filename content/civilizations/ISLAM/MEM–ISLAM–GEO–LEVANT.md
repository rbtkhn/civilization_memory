I. MEMORY PURPOSE & SCOPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This file preserves the Levant as Islam's **sacred-zone corridor and
legitimacy fulcrum**: the eastern Mediterranean littoral and hinterland
(Syria, Lebanon, Palestine, Jordan) containing Jerusalem/Al-Aqsa and Gaza—
a zone where Islamic legitimacy is territorially anchored and where
violation triggers DPI escalation non-linearly.

For Islam, the Levant is:
• Jerusalem/Al-Aqsa—third holiest site; Qur'anically sanctified
• Gaza—sacred-zone integrity; siege as legitimacy pressure
• Levantine corridor—linking Hijaz, Nile, Anatolia, Mesopotamia

"Eastern Med corridors → sacred memory activation accelerant."

II. GEOGRAPHIC PARAMETERS & STRUCTURAL LOGIC
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The Levant spans from the Taurus Mountains (north) to Sinai (south),
bounded by the Mediterranean (west) and the Syrian Desert (east). Core
components: coastal plain, mountain spine (Lebanon, Anti-Lebanon),
Jordan Rift Valley, inland plateaus.

Structural axiom:
> Sacred zones are NON-FUNGIBLE. Loss of Jerusalem/Al-Aqsa cannot be
> compensated by economics, development, or autonomy frameworks.

Terrain: Mixed—coastal, mountain, desert fringe. Climate: Mediterranean;
arid interior. Key cities: Jerusalem, Damascus, Aleppo, Beirut, Gaza.

III. SACRED-ZONE LOGIC & POWER DISTRIBUTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Jerusalem/Al-Aqsa functions as a **legitimacy anchor**—not a chokepoint
in the maritime sense, but a node whose status (INTACT / PRESSURED /
VIOLATED) governs DPI and FRG across the Ummah.

Power distribution implications:
• Control of Jerusalem: Legitimacy claim; custody confers authority
• Gaza: Siege/closure compresses urban import physics; DPI accelerator
• External occupation: Sacred-zone violation → legitimacy rebinding
  accelerates against enforcing authority (CIV–CORE § II)

Defense balance: Sacred-zone defense is non-negotiable under high DPI;
deterrence logic is overridden when dignity or sacred integrity is
threatened (Dignity Supremacy Law).
