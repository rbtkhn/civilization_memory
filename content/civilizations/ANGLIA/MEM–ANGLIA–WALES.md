I. MEMORY PURPOSE & SCOPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This file preserves Wales as **Anglia’s first integrated periphery**:
a region absorbed early through conquest and law, whose identity
survives culturally while governance is fully Anglian.

Wales is incorporated, not federated.

Contradictions are preserved without synthesis.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
II. EARLY INCORPORATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Wales is integrated before Anglia fully matures.

Characteristics:
• Military conquest
• Legal assimilation
• Administrative standardization

Resistance is overcome structurally.

Law replaces force.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
III. CULTURAL CONTINUITY WITHOUT POLITICAL AUTONOMY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Welsh identity persists.

Forms:
• Language
• Poetry
• Local traditions

Political sovereignty does not.

Culture decouples from statehood.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IV. RESOURCE & LABOR CONTRIBUTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Wales supports Anglia materially.

Roles:
• Mining
• Industrial labor
• Military manpower

Economic function outweighs political voice.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
V. SYMBOLIC FUNCTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Wales becomes normalized.

Effects:
• No recurring secession logic
• Limited strategic anxiety
• Accepted incorporation

Early absorption prevents later instability.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VI. RELATION TO ANGLIAN CORE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Wales:
• Does not shape Anglian doctrine
• Does not contest legitimacy
• Supplies continuity and depth

Integration is complete.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VII. CIVILIZATIONAL MEMORY FUNCTION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Wales encodes **successful early consolidation**.

Lessons:
• Early integration reduces future conflict.
• Cultural survival does not require sovereignty.
• Law can pacify permanently.

Wales is Anglia’s quiet interior.
