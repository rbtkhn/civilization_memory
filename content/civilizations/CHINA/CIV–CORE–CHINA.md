I. CIVILIZATIONAL IDENTITY & PRIME AXIOMS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Continuity Civilization (LOCKED)
China is a civilizational organism that absorbs dynasties, ideologies,
religions, technologies, and regimes into a continuous substrate rather
than resetting. Collapse is metabolized into memory, not termination.

2. Mandate-of-Heaven Legitimacy Logic
Authority derives from performance: order, material provisioning,
stability, and civilizational dignity. Legitimacy is revocable by failure,
not elections, charisma, or ideology.

3. Centralized Absorption Doctrine
Plurality is tolerated only if it can be standardized, harmonized, or
hierarchically integrated into the civilizational core.

4. Harmony-over-Freedom Axiom
Predictability, order, and equilibrium are valued above individual
autonomy or adversarial pluralism. Disorder is a civilizational failure
condition.

5. Civilization-as-Administration Principle
China governs through scale, repetition, bureaucracy, and procedural
continuity rather than revolutionary rupture or personal rule.
