III. CRITICAL OBSERVATION: TEMPORAL IMBALANCE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The corpus exhibits significant reliance on ARC-T-MODERN sources.

STRUCTURAL CAUSE:
Russia lacks an extensive classical literary tradition comparable
to Rome or Greece. Medieval Russian sources (chronicles, hagiography)
exist but require specialized scholarly mediation.

MITIGATION:
• ARC-T-EARLY-MOD authors (Klyuchevsky, Solovyov, Karamzin) serve
  as foundational interpretive authorities
• Their temporal precedence over ARC-T-MODERN is ENFORCED

This is NOT a compliance failure but a civilizational characteristic.
