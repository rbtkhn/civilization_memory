Day-by-day record of **events** and **analysis** for the US–Iran conflict (war phase from 1 March 2026). Append-only. Use for quick lookup of what happened on a given day and how the session read it.

### 2026-03-02 · Day 2
**Events:** Putin offers to help restore calm; speaks with UAE, Bahrain, Qatar. Iran continues strikes; Gulf invokes Art 51. Pentagon first detailed briefing; Rubio briefs House and Senate Intelligence.
**Trump (verbatim):** (Truth Social video, Sunday afternoon) Declared "sweeping battlefield gains" and the death of Iran's Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei. "Three US military service members have been killed in action … and sadly there will likely be more before it ends." — (Truth Social, reported) "Khamenei, one of the most evil people in History, is dead. This is not only Justice for the people of Iran, but for all Great Americans... The heavy and pinpoint bombing, however, will continue, uninterrupted."
**Hegseth (verbatim):** (Pentagon briefing, Monday 2 Mar) "We didn't start this war, but under President [Donald] Trump, we're finishing it." Iran waging a "savage, one-sided war against America" for 47 years. "They didn't always declare it openly, except for their constant chants of 'death to America.' They did it through the blood of our people, car bombs in Beirut, rocket attacks on our ships, murders at our embassies, roadside bombs in Iraq and Afghanistan, funded and armed by Iranian Quds Force and IRGC killers." "This is not a so-called regime change war, but the regime sure did change, and the world is better off for it." "To the media outlets and political left screaming 'endless wars!' Stop. This is not Iraq. This is not endless. I was there for both. Our generation knows better, and so does this president." "No stupid rules of engagement, no nation-building quagmire, no democracy-building exercise, no politically correct wars. We fight to win, and we don't waste time or lives." "We are not going into the exercise of [saying] what we will or will not do. President Trump ensures that our enemies understand we'll go as far as we need to go to advance American interests." "But we're not dumb about it. You don't have to roll 200,000 people in there and stay 20 years." "To the people of Iran, this is your moment."
**Rubio (verbatim):** (Capitol Hill, 2 Mar, after Intelligence briefings) "The hardest hits are yet to come." "The objective of this mission is to destroy their ballistic missile capability and make sure they can't rebuild it." "What they are trying to do…is build a conventional weapons capability as a shield where they can hide behind." "There absolutely was an imminent threat." "The president determined we were not going to get hit first. It's that simple." "We would love for there to be an Iran that's not governed by radical Shia clerics." "The objective of this mission is the destruction of their ballistic missile capabilities."
**Sources:** Boston Globe 2 Mar 2026; Foreign Policy 2 Mar 2026; Military.com 2 Mar 2026; NHPR; Sahm Capital / Business Insider (Truth Social archive).
