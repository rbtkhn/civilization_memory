Expansion is disfavored; cost-imposition, deniability, swarm logic,
and multi-domain friction dominate.

7. Shi’a Memory Engine
Martyrdom → endurance → legitimacy → sovereignty recursion.
Memory functions as operational software.

Prosperity is optional; sovereignty and endurance are mandatory.
