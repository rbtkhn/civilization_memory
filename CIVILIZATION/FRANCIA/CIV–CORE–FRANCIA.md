────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I. CIVILIZATIONAL IDENTITY & PRIME AXIOMS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Universalist Legitimacy Civilization (LOCKED)
Francia is a civilization that seeks endurance through the projection
of *universal principles*—law, rights, reason, and moral authority—
rather than through ethnic cohesion, administrative depth, or sheer force.

2. Moral–Legal Legitimacy Logic
Authority is tolerated insofar as it is perceived to embody justice,
reason, and civilizational leadership. Legitimacy decays when declared
principles diverge from observable outcomes.

3. Expression-Over-Restraint Bias
Francia prioritizes declarative clarity, moral positioning, and symbolic
action over procedural quietism or delayed execution.

4. Power-by-Norm Projection Axiom
Power is generated by defining standards others are pressured to adopt:
legal norms, cultural language, diplomatic framing, and ethical reference
points.

5. Civilization-as-Arbiter Principle
Francia operates most naturally as judge, mediator, or moral reference
rather than as administrator or enforcer.
