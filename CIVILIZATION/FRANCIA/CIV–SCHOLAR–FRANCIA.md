I. SCHOLAR PURPOSE & ROLE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CIV–SCHOLAR–FRANCIA is the cumulative learning ledger of the
Francia civilization.

It is not an analyst.
It is not a strategist.
It is not predictive.
It does not reason independently.

Its function is to record how Francia learns:
• What was ingested
• In what order
• What beliefs were extracted
• How those beliefs were synthesized
• Which beliefs were frozen into doctrine

Scholar preserves epistemic history.
CIV–CORE governs interpretation.
