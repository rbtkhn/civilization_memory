────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I. MEMORY PURPOSE & SCOPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This file preserves Richard I as **Anglia’s archetypal absent king**:
a ruler of extraordinary martial prestige whose identity and legitimacy
are forged almost entirely outside the institutional life of Anglia.

Richard embodies glory without governance.

Contradictions are preserved without synthesis.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
II. KINGSHIP AS MARTIAL PERSONA
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Richard’s authority is personal and performative.

Characteristics:
• Warrior-king ideal
• Chivalric excellence
• Reputation-based legitimacy
• Minimal domestic administration

Rule is expressed through battle, not procedure.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
III. THE CRUSADER KING
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The Third Crusade defines Richard’s memory.

Effects:
• Pan-European fame
• Military parity with Saladin
• Enduring heroic mythology

Crusading success elevates Richard—
but not Anglia’s institutions.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IV. ABSENCE FROM ANGLIA
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Richard spends little time governing.

Consequences:
• Delegated rule
• Fiscal extraction to fund wars
• Administrative strain without reform

Anglia tolerates absence because institutions endure.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
V. CIVILIZATIONAL MISALIGNMENT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Richard’s values diverge from Anglian logic.

Contrasts:
• Glory vs continuity
• Personal rule vs procedure
• Continental identity vs island governance

Richard is admired—but not emulated.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VI. FAILURE TO TRANSMIT A MODEL
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Richard leaves no lasting governance template.

Outcomes:
• No institutional legacy
• No procedural innovation
• No dynastic stabilization

His legend survives.
His system does not.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VII. RETROSPECTIVE SYMBOLISM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Later memory reframes Richard selectively.

Uses:
• National romance
• Chivalric exemplar
• Cultural hero detached from policy

He becomes myth without mandate.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
VIII. LONG-TERM CIVILIZATIONAL SIGNIFICANCE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Richard clarifies Anglia’s trajectory by contrast.

Lessons:
• Heroic kingship is optional
• Institutions matter more than brilliance
• Absence tests—but does not break—procedure

Anglia survives Richard because it does not become him.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IX. CONTINUITY INSIGHTS (RAW)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Personal glory does not scale institutionally.
• Absence reveals procedural resilience.
• Chivalric kingship is a dead end for Anglia.
• Heroic memory can coexist with systemic rejection.
• Richard defines what Anglia chooses not to be.

Tension preserved without verdict.
