I. MEMORY PURPOSE & SCOPE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This file preserves Trafalgar as **Anglia’s naval point of no return**:
the battle that irreversibly secured maritime supremacy and rendered
continental invasion structurally impossible for the duration of the
Napoleonic era.

Trafalgar is not a victory of opportunity.
It is a victory of system maturity.

Contradictions are preserved without synthesis.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Trafalgar occurs amid existential threat.

Context:

• Anglia’s reliance on naval denial for survival

→ Control the sea or risk annihilation.
