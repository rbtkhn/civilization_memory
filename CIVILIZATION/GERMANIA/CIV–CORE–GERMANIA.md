no longer restrains ambition, but amplifies it.

Indicators:
• Success interpreted as moral validation
• Institutions repurposed for expansion
• War redefined from instrument to destiny
• Political caution reframed as weakness

Once the ceiling is breached:
→ Discipline accelerates collapse instead of preventing it.
