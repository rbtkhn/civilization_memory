I. CIVILIZATIONAL IDENTITY & PRIME AXIOMS (REAFFIRMED)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Depth-Through-Organization Civilization (LOCKED)
Germania produces endurance through process, repetition, and institutional
depth rather than charisma, ideology, or narrative unity.

2. Competence-Derived Legitimacy Logic
Authority persists while systems deliver reliability, provisioning, and
functional order. Legitimacy collapses through performance failure, not
moral judgment.

3. Replacement-Before-Expansion Doctrine
Power is generated by the ability to replace personnel, materiel, capital,
and procedure faster than loss is incurred.

4. Order-Over-Narrative Axiom
Predictability and procedural correctness supersede mythic cohesion.
Narrative follows process, never leads it.

5. Civilization-as-Administration Principle
Germania governs through rule-bound administration and coalition-managed
execution, not ideological command.
