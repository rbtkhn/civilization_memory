────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I. CIVILIZATIONAL IDENTITY & PRIME AXIOMS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Depth-Through-Organization Civilization (LOCKED)
Germania is a civilizational organism that produces endurance through
process, repetition, and institutional depth rather than charisma,
ideology, or narrative unity.

2. Competence-Derived Legitimacy Logic
Authority is tolerated so long as systems deliver reliability,
material provisioning, and functional order. Legitimacy is revoked by
failure of performance rather than moral judgment or electoral signal.

3. Replacement-Before-Expansion Doctrine
Power is generated by the capacity to replace personnel, equipment,
capital, and procedures faster than loss is incurred. Expansion without
replacement depth is treated as strategic error.

4. Order-Over-Narrative Axiom
Predictability, procedure, and technical correctness are valued above
expressive politics or mythic cohesion. Narrative is secondary to
process credibility.

5. Civilization-as-Administration Principle
Germania governs through rule-bound administration, legal continuity,
and coalition-managed execution rather than centralized ideological
command.
